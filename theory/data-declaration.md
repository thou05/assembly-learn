- **Declare variables:**
  - `s1 db 1` - `s1` is 1 byte, with value 1.
  - `s2 db ?` - `s2` is 1 byte, uninitialized.
  - `s3 db 10 dup(?)` - Array of 10 elements, uninitialized.

  - `s5 db 'thao xinh gai$'` - String type, ends with `$`.
